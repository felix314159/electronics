# Wiring

## ATtiny85

Pin 8:  +5 V
Pin 4:  GND
Pin 6:  1 kΩ:  transistor base
Pin 7:  potentiometer wiper

## Potentiometer

outer leg 1:  +5 V
outer leg 2:  GND
middle leg:  ATtiny Pin 7

## Transistor (2N2222)
base:  1 kΩ from ATtiny Pin 6
emitter:  GND
collector:  fan blue wire

## Fan (Sunon MagLev MF60070V1)
red:  +5 V
black:  GND
blue:  transistor collector [pwm control]
yellow:  not connected [tachometer / FG output not needed here]

## Capacitors
100 nF between ATtiny Pin 8 and Pin 4
optional 10-100 µF between +5 V and GND near fan

## Power Supply
3x AA Alkaline in series
